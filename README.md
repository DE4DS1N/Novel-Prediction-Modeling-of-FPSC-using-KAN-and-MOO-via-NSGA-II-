# Novel Prediction Modeling of Flat Plate Solar Collector using Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks (KAN) and Multi-Objective Performance Optimization via NSGA-II

This study introduces a high-fidelity framework for the design optimization of Flat Plate Solar Collectors (FPSC). By combining Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), interpretable Machine Learning, and Evolutionary Algorithms, we provide a transparent and efficient methodology for renewable energy systems.

### Key Contributions:
* **High-Fidelity Dataset:** Generated from 1,000 CFD simulations across 8 design parameters.
* **Interpretable ML:** Implemented Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks (KAN), which outperformed conventional MLP models with an $R^2$ of 0.992 for outlet temperature.
* **Symbolic Regression:** Derived explicit "glass-box" analytical formulas from pruned KAN models for real-world engineering use.
* **Multi-Objective Optimization:** Integrated KAN with NSGA-II to identify a Pareto-optimal knee point at ~140 W heat transfer and ~2.5 Pa pressure drop.
